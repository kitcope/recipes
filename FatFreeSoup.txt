Name: Fat Free Soup

Precis: Useful for slimming.

Serves: 4

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:			Ingredients

					3 large onions
					1 large green pepper
					1 head of celery
					2 tins of tomatos
					0.5 cabbage
					0.5 packet of onion soup mix
					3 vegetable stock cubes


Method:				Method

					Peel onions and de-seed pepper.
					Roughly chop all vegetables
					Place all ingredients in pan and add water to cover.
					Boil for 15 minutes.
					Liquidise in food processor.
					Return to pan to re-heat if necessary.

					

Service: Season as necessary and serve hot.
