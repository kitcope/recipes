Easy Broccoli Quiche

Easy but impressive vegetarian dish.

Serves
Preparation: 20.0 minutes Start:
Cooking: 30.0 minutes End:

Ingredients for pastry base   
200.0 grams plain flour
115.0 grams fat ( butter or lard combination)
1.0 egg

Ingredients for filling
3.0 tbsp butter
1.0 onion
2.0 cloves garlic
450.0 ml fresh broccoli
350.0 ml cheddar cheese
4.0 eggs
250.0 ml milk
1.0 tsp salt
0.5 tsp ground black pepper

Method for base 
Preheat oven to 180.0ºC.
Combine pastry ingredients adding a little water if necessary
Roll out and line 25.0 cm diameter oven proof dish

Method for filling
Preheat oven to 190.0ºC.
Crush garlic in garlic press.
Grate cheese.
Beat eggs.
Finely chop onion.
Melt 2.0 tbsp butter in lidded saucepan over low heat.
Add onion, garlic and broccoli then close lid.
Cook slowly, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are soft.
Spoon vegetables onto base and sprinkle with cheese.
Whisk to combine eggs and milk, seasoning well.
Melt remaining 1.0 tbsp butter and stir into egg mixture.
Pour combined mixture over vegetables and cheese.
Bake for 30 minutes until centre has set.

Service: Serve hot or cold.
