Roast pork in white wine

Light and easy main course.

Serves
Preparation: 10.0 minutes Start:
Cooking: 1.3 hours End:

Ingredients   
1.5 kg loin of pork, boned and skinned.
2.0 tsp fennel seeds
2.0 tbsp butter
1.0 tbsp olive oil
8.0 cloves of garlic ( whole - unpeeled)
1.0 sprig rosemary
4.0 bay leaves
1.0 bulb of fennel
375.0 ml white wine ( Chardonnay or similar)

Method 
Preheat oven to 200.0ºC.
Roll and tie up meat with string.
Season outside generously.
Roll meat in fennel seeds.
Heat 1.0 tbsp of butter with olive oil in roasting tray.
Fry off meat for 2 minutes until golden colour.
Slice fennel bulb and add with garlic, herbs and wine.
Cover with wet greaseproof paper and place in oven for 1 hour.
Rest meat on plate.
Add remaining 1.0 tbsp butter to cooking juices to form sauce.
Scrape any goodness from tray base and crush garlic cloves.
Strain sauce before serving if desired.
