Fat Free Soup

Useful for slimming.

Serves
Preparation: 15.0 minutes Start:
Cooking: 15.0 minutes End:

Ingredients   
3.0 large onions
1.0 large green pepper
1.0 head of celery
2.0 tins of tomatos
0.5 cabbage
0.5 packet of onion soup mix
3.0 vegetable stock cubes

Method 
Peel onions and de-seed pepper.
Roughly chop all vegetables
Place all ingredients in pan and add water to cover.
Boil for 15 minutes.
Liquidise in food processor.
Return to pan to re-heat if necessary.

Service: Season as necessary and serve hot.
