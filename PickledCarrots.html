Pickled Carrots

Crunchy pickle to accompany salads etc.

Serves
Preparation: 10.0 minutes Start:
Cooking: 5.0 minutes End:

Ingredients  Highlight  
2.0 small clean empty coffee jars
300.0 grams carrots
250.0 ml water
100.0 ml wine vinegar
25.0 grams sugar
0.5 tsp salt
1.0 tsp cumin seeds
0.5 tsp ground black pepper
1.0 bay leaf ( torn in half)

Method Change  
Peel and slice carrots diagonally into strips 3 - 4.0 cm long
Boil water and blanch carrots for 1 minute, cooling them immediately.
Add all other ingredients to water and boil for 2 minutes.
Place carrots in jars.
Allow pickling fluid to cool then top up jars.
Store in refrigerator.

Service: Serve cold, at least an hour after making.
