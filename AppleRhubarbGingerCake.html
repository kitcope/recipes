Apple, Rhubarb and ginger cake

A seasonal moist spicy afternoon cake.

Serves
Preparation: 25.0 minutes Start:
Cooking: 1.25 hours End:

Ingredients   
250.0 grams self raising flour
1.0 tbsp baking powder
150.0 grams butter
170.0 grams light brown sugar
3.0 medium eggs
2.0 tbsp honey or syrup
200.0 grams rhubarb
200.0 grams apple
3.0 tbsp ginger

Method 
Preheat oven to 180.0ºC.
Chop rhubarb, peel and finely chop ginger.
Peel, core and chop apples into bowl of acidulated water.
Cream butter and sugar together in mixer.
Whisk in eggs individually with honey or syrup.
Stir in flour, baking powder, ginger and chopped fruit.
Pour resulting gloopy mixture into cake tin.
Bake for 1.25 hours.

Service: Surprisingly good with marmalade!
