Green Bean Soup

Useful when there is a glut of beans.

Serves
Preparation: 15.0 minutes Start:
Cooking: 40.0 minutes End:

Ingredients  Highlight  
2.0 tbsp butter
20.0 runner beans or equivalent
3.0 onions
1.0 carrot
700.0 ml water
1.0 vegetable stock cube

Method Change  
Remove any strings and thinly slice beans.
Chop onions and carrot.
Melt butter and fry beans, onion and carrot until onion soft but not browned.
Add water and stock cube and bring to boil.
Cover and simmer gently for 30 minutes until vegetables are tender.
Liquidise, return soup to pan, season and reheat if necessary.

Service: Serve hot.
